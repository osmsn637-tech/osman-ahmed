# Adjust Item Training Script

## Goal
Teach workers the exact adjust-item flow using the real app screen.

## Timing
- 0:00 to 0:04: Review item details
- 0:04 to 0:08: Select location
- 0:08 to 0:12: Enter quantity
- 0:12 to 0:16: Confirm adjustment
- 0:16 to 0:20: Success message

## Narration
Review the item details and choose the location you want to adjust.
Enter the new quantity for that location, then tap Confirm.
When the success message appears, the adjustment is complete.

## On-Screen Labels
- Step 1: Review the item details and locations
- Step 2: Select the location to adjust
- Step 3: Enter the new quantity
- Step 4: Tap confirm to submit the adjustment
- Step 5: When the success message appears, the adjustment is complete
